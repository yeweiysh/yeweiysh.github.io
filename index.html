Short Bio

I am a tenure-track professor with the College of Electronic and Information Engineering and State Key Laboratory of Autonomous Intelligent Unmanned Systems at Tongji University. I worked as a postdoctoral researcher with Prof. Ambuj Singh on Human-AI team decision making and network science at the University of California, Santa Barbara, CA, USA. Before that, I was a researcher with the AI Platform Department at Tencent Corporation, Shenzhen, China, working on the game AI. I did my PhD at Ludwig-Maximilian University of Munich, Germany, where I was fortunately advised by Prof. Christian Böhm. I was a visiting PhD student with the iKDD research group at Helmholtz Zentrum Munich, where I worked with Prof. Claudia Plant.

Research

My research mainly focuses on machine learning, data mining, embodied multimodal LLMs (vision-language-action models), and 3D generation.
